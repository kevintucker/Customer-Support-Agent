# Recording Tips

1. **Audio**: Use a good mic, quiet room
2. **Screen**: 1920x1080, clean desktop
3. **Pacing**: Pause slightly between sections
4. **Energy**: Be enthusiastic but professional
5. **Backup**: Pre-type the chat messages to avoid typos on camera

---

# Key Messages to Emphasize

1. **"Resolves, not just responds"** — This is the differentiator
2. **"Smart escalation"** — AI knows its limits
3. **"Built in 7 days with Kiro"** — Spec-driven development
4. **"70% of tickets automated"** — The magic number
5. **"$15 saved per ticket"** — ROI that sells itself
